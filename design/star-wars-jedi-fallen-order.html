Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order Analysis

Game Design Article

This piece examines the basic melee StormTrooper enemy type from Star Wars: Jedi Fallen Order, using a structured approach to enemy design analysis.

Enemy Behavior Categories

Aggression

The StormTrooper has approximately 1.5-2 seconds of downtime between attacks.

Movement Speed

Enemies walk at player speed but use a lunging move to close distance more quickly.

Attack Range

Features short-range melee strikes plus one longer-range thrusting attack.

Attack Speed

Attacks have brief anticipation windows, making player counterattacks difficult.

Anticipation Pose/Readability

Attack windup animations are visually distinct and telegraph attack direction, allowing player prediction.

Attack Recovery

Most attacks include roughly 1 second of recovery time.

Attack Combo/Moveset

The enemy repertoire includes basic slashing combos, thrusting moves, and slam attacks.

Attack Tracking

Low tracking after attack initiation, though weapon collision remains large enough to catch nearby players.

Group Aggression

Multiple enemies coordinate attacks through token/threshold systems, preventing simultaneous strikes.

Player Damage

A two-hit combo removes approximately one-quarter of player health.

Player Reactions

Blocking triggers light knockback; unblocked attacks cause extended stagger effects.

Health

StormTroopers die in 2-3 hits and become vulnerable to execution moves when staggered.

Defense/Reactions

The game features a block stamina system where attacks drain defensive meters before causing stagger reactions.

Difficulty Note

Parry windows narrow significantly on higher difficulties, requiring precise timing.
